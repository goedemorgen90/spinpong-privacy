# Privacy Policy for SpinPong

**Last Updated:** 3 november 2025

## Introduction
Welcome to SpinPong. This privacy policy explains what information we collect, use, and share when you play our game. The short answer is: **we collect nothing.**

## Data Collection and Usage
SpinPong is a completely offline game. We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal or sensitive user data.

- **No Personal Information:** We do not ask for or collect any personal information such as your name, email address, location, or device identifiers.
- **No Gameplay Data:** All game progress, scores, and settings are stored locally on your device only and are never transmitted to us or any third party.
- **No Analytics:** We do not use any analytics tools to track your behavior or usage of the app.
- **No Advertising:** The app does not contain any third-party advertisements that could collect data.

## Permissions
SpinPong does not request any permissions to access your device's data or features.

## Changes to This Policy
We may update this privacy policy in the future if the app's functionality changes. Any updates will be posted on this page.
